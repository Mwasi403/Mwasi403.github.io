About LumiMinds

Your trusted partner in premium vehicle import solutions since 2018

Our Journey

Founded in 2018, Lumiminds emerged from a simple observation: importing vehicles to Zambia was often complicated, expensive, and filled with uncertainty. Our founders, experienced in international trade and automotive industries, saw an opportunity to revolutionize this process.

What started as a small operation helping friends and family import their dream vehicles has grown into Zambia's most trusted vehicle import service. We've successfully imported over 500 vehicles and built a reputation for transparency, reliability, and exceptional customer service.

Our Mission

To make premium vehicle ownership accessible to everyone in Zambia by providing transparent, reliable, and cost-effective import solutions. We strive to eliminate the complexities and uncertainties of vehicle importing through our expertise and dedication to customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become Zambia's most trusted and preferred vehicle import partner, recognized for our integrity, innovation, and commitment to delivering exceptional value. We envision a future where anyone can easily access their dream vehicle from anywhere in the world.

Our Values

Transparency in all our dealings, integrity in every transaction, excellence in service delivery, innovation in our processes, and commitment to our clients' satisfaction. These values form the foundation of everything we do at Lumiminds.
