About LumiMinds

LumiMinds is a Zambian multi-service company that has revolutionized how individuals and businesses access global products and technology solutions. Founded in 2018, we've grown from a specialized vehicle import service to a comprehensive solutions provider.

Our name reflects our approach - 'Lumi' meaning light, representing clarity and transparency, and 'Minds' representing our intelligent, strategic approach to solving complex challenges for our clients.

Today, we serve hundreds of clients across Zambia, providing everything from premium vehicle imports to cutting-edge technology solutions, all guided by our core values of integrity, excellence, and customer-centricity.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses in Zambia with comprehensive, reliable solutions that bridge global opportunities with local needs. We are committed to making premium vehicle ownership, cutting-edge technology, and digital innovation accessible through transparent processes and exceptional service.

Through our integrated approach, we eliminate complexities in vehicle imports, technology adoption, and digital transformation, ensuring our clients receive end-to-end support that exceeds expectations.

Our Vision

To become Zambia's most trusted multi-service partner, recognized for transforming how individuals and businesses access global products and digital solutions. We envision a future where geographical boundaries no longer limit access to quality vehicles, advanced technology, or innovative digital services.

By 2030, we aim to be the leading integrated service provider in Southern Africa, setting new standards for reliability, innovation, and customer-centric solutions across all our service domains.

Our Journey

2018

Foundation

LumiMinds was founded with a focus on revolutionizing vehicle imports to Zambia. We started with a small team and a big vision to make premium vehicles accessible to all Zambians.

2019

Rapid Growth

Within our first year, we successfully imported over 50 vehicles and established partnerships with international suppliers. Our reputation for reliability and transparency began to grow.

2020

Service Expansion

Recognizing our clients' broader needs, we expanded into gadget imports and IT support services, establishing ourselves as a comprehensive solutions provider.

2022

Digital Transformation

We launched our web development and mobile app services, helping businesses establish and grow their digital presence. This marked our evolution into a full-service technology partner.

2024

Market Leadership

Today, we're proud to have served hundreds of clients across Zambia, with over 500 vehicles imported and numerous successful technology projects completed.
