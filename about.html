About LumiMinds

Your trusted partner for comprehensive solutions - from vehicle imports to cutting-edge technology services

Our Journey

Founded in 2018, Lumiminds emerged from a simple observation: importing vehicles to Zambia was often complicated, expensive, and filled with uncertainty. Our founders, experienced in international trade and automotive industries, saw an opportunity to revolutionize this process.

What started as a small operation helping friends and family import their dream vehicles has grown into Zambia's most trusted multi-service provider. We've successfully expanded our expertise beyond vehicle imports to include technology solutions, gadget sourcing, and digital services.
