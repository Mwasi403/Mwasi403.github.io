Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does the import process take?

The complete process typically takes 4-6 weeks from vehicle selection to delivery, depending on the source country and shipping method.

What documents do I need?

We handle most documentation. You'll mainly need a valid ID, proof of address, and import duty payment. We guide you through everything.

Can I inspect the vehicle before purchase?

Yes! We provide detailed inspection reports, photos, and can arrange video calls for virtual inspections before you commit.

What about warranty and after-sales support?

We offer 3-month aftercare support and can help source spare parts. Many vehicles come with remaining manufacturer warranty.
